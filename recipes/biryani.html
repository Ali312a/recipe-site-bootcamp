Biryani Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g chicken
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 onions, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tbsp biryani masala
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 2 tbsp ghee
  • Salt to taste

Preparation Time:

60 minutes

Instructions:

Cook rice until 70% done. In another pot, saut√© onions in ghee until golden. Add chicken, spices, yogurt, and tomatoes. Layer the partially cooked rice over the chicken mixture. Cover and cook on low heat for 20 minutes. Serve with raita.
