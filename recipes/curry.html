Curry Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken
  • 2 cups coconut milk
  • 1 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste

Preparation Time:

40 minutes

Instructions:

Heat oil in a pan and saut√© onions and garlic until golden. Add chicken and cook until browned. Mix in curry powder and stir for 2 minutes. Add coconut milk and simmer for 20 minutes. Serve hot with rice.
